## Brand Story

Founded in **1985** by **Hamid Filali** in Casablanca, Filali Design Co. began as an artisan workshop built on word-of-mouth reputation. Over 35+ years, it has grown into a recognized name in Moroccan luxury interior design — now led by a second generation of the Filali family. The brand bridges ancestral Moroccan craftsmanship with contemporary design sensibility.

### Key Milestones

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 1985 | Workshop founded by Hamid Filali in Casablanca |
| 1990s | Reputation grows via word-of-mouth |
| 2000s | Expansion to villas, restaurants, and commercial spaces |
| 2010s | Regional expansion across Morocco |
| Today | Digital transformation with international reach |

### By the Numbers

- **35+** years of craftsmanship
- **500+** projects completed
- **2** generations of leadership
- Coverage: Casablanca → all of Morocco → international

### Description

The logo is a **stylized bilingual monogram** that fuses the Latin "F" with the Arabic "فا" (Fa). It is built on an **8-point Islamic geometric star** base, surrounded by decorative rings with layered opacity. The mark is adaptive (`currentColor`) — rendering correctly on both light and dark surfaces. The logo carries an Arabic tagline and a dashed horizontal decorative line as a secondary element.

- **ViewBox:** 100×100
- **Style:** Geometric / Heritage / Calligraphic
- **Aria Label:** "Filali Design Co. Logo"
